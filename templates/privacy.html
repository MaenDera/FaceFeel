Privacy Policy

Your Data & GDPR

This app analyzes uploaded images entirely in-memory to estimate emotions using AI. Your image is never stored, logged, or shared.

What’s Processed?

  • Temporary image data (held in memory)
  • Emotion probabilities derived from detected faces

Data Storage

No data is saved. Processing ends when your prediction is shown. No backups, no logs, no tracking — ever.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You can ask about processing at any time — though we don’t keep any of your data once your results are shown.
