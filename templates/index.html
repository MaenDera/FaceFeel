Face Feel

Before Use:

You can upload a photo with one or more faces. Now for best results:


  • ✅ Use clear, front-facing images.
  • 👥 If the image has many faces or the faces are small, use "Find Faces".
  • 👤 If the image already shows a single, close-up face, leave it unchecked.
  • 🔍 Click Reveal Emotion and wait for the results.
  • 🔐 Your image is never saved. Everything happens temporarily in memory.
  • ⚠️ Note: This is an AI-based system and may not always interpret emotions accurately, especially in low-quality images or complex expressions.

✨ Pro Tips ✨

  • Dig up an old photos, maybe try using a photo from your CV.
  • Use extreme expressions; big laughs or tears.
  • Try bright sunlight, dim lamps, and side lighting.
  • Upload both group shots and close ups.
  • Shoot from different angles.
  • Compare neutral vs. happy portraits of the same face.
  • Include different eras.
